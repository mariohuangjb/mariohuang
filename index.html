In 2008, I completed my Bachelor degree from the University of Alberta in pure mathematics. In the same year, I started my graduate study in algebraic combinatorics at the department of Combinatorics and Optimization in the University of Waterloo. After completing my master degree under the supervision of David Wagner in 2009, I completed my Ph.D. in 2014 under the supervision of Chris Godsil.

My research activities can be found here.

While I enjoy thinking about interesting problems in mathematics, I am also passionate in teaching.

Hoping to introduce some elementary topics in mathematics while also trying to have some fun, I wrote some articles here.
