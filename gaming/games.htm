Gaming is something that I have been interested in since I was a kid. When I was a kindergartner, I played my first ever video game, which was either Super Mario Bros. or Contra on the Famicom (or NES in Amerian term), though I don't remember which one exactly I played first.

Diablo, which I first played in 1997, was the game that first introduced to me many role-playing game elements, such as level-up, quests, equipments and spells. Being the game that changed my perspective to video games in general, it remains one of my all-time favourites.
